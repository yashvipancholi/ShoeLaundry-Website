What Our Website Offer ?

Complete Shoe Care: Cleaning, Repair & Protection. Whether you need shoe cleaning, shoe protection you can get the best possible services right here.

SHOE CLEANING

We deep clean every single part of your shoe including laces, soles & straps. we use different shoe cleaners and techniques depending on the material of footwear. We always work on a small area for test cleaning and ensure that the process will not damage items. Keeping the WHO & CDC hygiene standards in consideration, we focus on sanitization as well. Lastly, we use German deodorizers to kill unwanted odour.

SHOE REPAIR & RESTORATION

To combat the effects of wear and tear or other damages your shoes need proper repair. We have expertise in reversing every type of damage your shoe can ever have. You can get heel height altered, the colour changed, tassels added, and much more. Basically, you can get any customization you wish and give a new look and feel to your old shoes.

SHOE PROTECTION & NOURISHMENT

It is crucial to protect shoes from water, dust, dirt, and stains. For this purpose, we apply a thin transparent protective coating of Mink & Lanolin oil. With prolonged usage and exposure to dirt, light-coloured shoes (especially white ones) turn pale yellowish. We use special chemicals to deoxidize and boost the white colour to get your shoes shining again.

ANY SHOE TYPE, FABRIC & BRAND

From canvas shoes to heels, and from velvet to nylon shoe fabrics, we can handle all types of shoes. We also have expertise in handling luxury shoes from brands like Gucci, Louis Vuitton and Christian Louboutin.
